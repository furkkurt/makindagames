After getting into Firat University which is known for it's success in informatics related programs, he kept on working on front-end web and mobile game development jobs, founded makinDAGames and started publishing games on Google Play Store under that name. And it's the goal to get all the mobile games ported to be played on web, here on this site. Work in progress boys!

He also studied one year in Spain so he is very international. Who knows more, the one who travels much or the one who reads much? Well no worries cause Furkan is on both tables. Check the goodreads profile on the contacts page if you don't take my word.

The name "makinDAGames" comes with many factors, first of it means "making the games" of course which is what we do, the DAG in the middle in uppercase is a nickname he used around the internet, meaning "mountain" in turkish, that's the reason we have a mountain on the logo, and finally makindagames when thought as a single word, is easy to pronounce for languages that are spoken exactly as written.
